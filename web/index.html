What is Guarden?

Guarden is an offline-first password vault built for people who want local control over sensitive login data. Unlike traditional cloud-based managers, Guarden prioritizes your privacy by keeping your data where it belongs: on your device.

Core Purpose

The application allows users to store web passwords, bank account credentials, subscription details, and private notes in one locally encrypted vault. It uses industry-standard AES-256 encryption and biometric authentication to ensure that only you can access your data.

Why Google OAuth?

Guarden uses Google OAuth solely for the optional "Encrypted Cloud Backup" feature. This allows you to securely upload your encrypted vault to your own Google Drive space (using the "drive.file" scope), giving you an extra layer of recovery without compromising our offline-first core philosophy.
