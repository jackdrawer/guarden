Privacy Policy for Guarden PW Manager

Last Updated: March 2026

Guarden PW Manager ("we", "app", or "service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

Guarden is an offline-first application. All your passwords, bank details, and notes are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your vault data.

2. Google OAuth & Drive Usage

If you choose to use the backup feature, the app will request access to your Google Drive ("drive.file" scope). This is used only to:

  • Upload your encrypted vault file to a specific folder in your Drive.
  • Download the vault file during a restore process.

The app only interacts with the specific files it creates and cannot access other files in your Google Drive. This access is essential for the backup and recovery functionality.

3. Security

All data is encrypted using AES-256 before being stored or uploaded. Your master password is never stored or transmitted.
