# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 2026

## 1. Introduction

Guarden PW Manager ("we", "app", or "service") takes your privacy extremely seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect from our app users, how we use, store, and protect this information.

By downloading or using our app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Locally Stored Data

Guarden PW Manager stores all passwords and sensitive data ** device**. The followinglocally on your are stored encrypted on your device:

- Website passwords
- Bank account information
- Subscription information
- User-added notes and custom fields

**Important:** We do not store any of this data on our servers. All data stays on your device and is only accessible to you.

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

The following technical data may be collected anonymously during app use:

- App usage statistics (including crash reports)
- Device type and operating system version
- Performance metrics

This data is used solely to improve app performance.

### 2.3 Optional Data

Additional data may be collected if you choose to use the following features:

- **Cloud Backup:** If you enable cloud backup, your encrypted data will be stored in your selected cloud service.
- **Biometric Authentication:** Your device's biometric sensor is used for fingerprint or facial recognition.

## 3. Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- Providing core app functions (password storage, auto-fill)
- Improving user experience
- Detecting and fixing errors
- Enhancing security features

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Encryption

All your sensitive data is:

- Protected with AES-256 industry-standard encryption algorithm
- Encrypted locally on your device
- Your master password never leaves your device

### 4.2 Biometric Protection

- Biometric authentication works with your device's security system
- Biometric data is never sent to our servers
- You can disable biometric authentication at any time

### 4.3 Local Storage

- All data is stored on your device
- Cloud synchronization is off by default
- Users can manually enable cloud backup

## 5. Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

- **Firebase Crashlytics:** For crash reports (anonymous data)
- **Cloud Providers:** For user-enabled backups

These service providers only process data according to our instructions and in accordance with our privacy policy.

## 6. Data Sharing

Guarden PW Manager:

- Does **not sell** your personal data
- Does **not share** sensitive data with third parties
- May only share data in legally required circumstances (court order, legal proceedings)

## 7. User Rights

Under GDPR (Europe) and KVKK (Turkey), you have the following rights:

- **Right to Access:** You can request information we hold about you
- **Right to Correction:** You can request correction of inaccurate data
- **Right to Deletion:** You can request deletion of your data
- **Right to Object:** You can object to certain data processing activities
- **Right to Data Portability:** You can receive your data in machine-readable format

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not designed for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
