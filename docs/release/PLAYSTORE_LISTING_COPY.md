### Turkish Full Description

```text
Guarden, hassas giris bilgilerini cihazinda tutmak isteyen kullanicilar icin gelistirilmis offline-first bir sifre kasasidir.

Web sifrelerini, banka hesap girislerini, abonelik bilgilerini ve ozel notlarini tek bir sifreli kasada toplayabilirsin. Kasani master sifrenle veya biyometri ile hizli sekilde acabilir, kayitlarini duzenli sekilde yonetebilir ve ihtiyac duydugunda sifreli yedek olusturabilirsin.

Guarden'in odak noktasi:

- Offline-first kullanim modeli
- Cihazda yerel ve sifreli veri saklama
- Parmak izi veya yuz tanima ile hizli giris
- Google Drive'a sifreli yedek yukleme
- Cihaza sifreli yedek alma ve geri yukleme
- Otomatik doldurma destegi
- Banka, abonelik ve web sifreleri icin duzenli yonetim
- Gizlilik odakli kullanim ve kontrollu telemetri yapisi

Neleri saklayabilirsin:

- Web site girisleri
- Banka ve finans hesap bilgileri
- Abonelik kayitlari
- Notlar ve ozel alanlar

Guvenlik modeli:

Guarden yerel sahiplik anlayisiyla tasarlandi. Kasa verilerin cihazinda sifreli olarak tutulur. Bulut yedekleme zorunlu degildir; istersen kendi Google Drive alaninda ek bir koruma katmani olarak kullanirsin. Biyometrik giris cihazinin guvenlik altyapisini kullanir. Sifreli yedekleri geri yuklemek icin dogru master sifre yine gerekir.

Eger bulut zorunlulugu olmayan, yerel sifreleme sunan ve yedekleme secenegini kontrolunde tutan bir sifre yoneticisi ariyorsan, Guarden bu model icin tasarlandi.
```

### English Play Title

`Guarden`

### English Short Description

`Offline-first password vault with biometrics, autofill, and encrypted backup.`

### English Full Description

```text
Guarden is an offline-first password vault built for people who want local control over sensitive login data.

Store web passwords, bank account credentials, subscription details, notes, and custom fields in one encrypted vault on your device. Unlock the vault with your master password or biometrics, keep records organized, and create encrypted backups when you need recovery protection.

What Guarden offers:

- Offline-first vault design
- Encrypted local storage for sensitive records
- Biometric unlock for faster access
- Google Drive backup with encrypted backup payloads
- Local encrypted backup export and restore
- Autofill support for faster sign-in workflows
- Separate areas for bank accounts, subscriptions, and web passwords
- Privacy-focused behavior with controlled telemetry options

What you can store:

- Website logins
- Bank and financial account credentials
- Subscription records
- Notes and custom fields

Security model:

Guarden is built around local ownership. Vault data stays on your device and is encrypted locally. Optional cloud backup is an extra recovery layer, not the default source of truth. Biometric unlock uses your device security system for convenience, and encrypted backups still require the correct master password when restoring.

If you want a password manager that prioritizes encrypted local storage, optional backup, and practical daily use without forcing a cloud-first workflow, Guarden is built for that model.
```
